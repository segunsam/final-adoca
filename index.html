ADETORO OMOOBA CARES HOME (ADOCAH) is a non governmental, non political, not - for – profit making and private organization (NGO) which put the welfare of the poor, the rejected, the motherless, physically and mentally challenged and the less-fortunate ones in our society as its paramount interest.
It is meant and solely established to cater, relief, rehabilitate, reform and to solicit assistance from public spirited individuals, institutions, private organizations and government for the less privileged ones without prejudice as regard to colour, tribe and religion.
ADOCAH focus on helping humanity and building others up in some ways through which the world can be a better place for us all to live in as no one person can live without another and according to the law of nature which stipulates that “ you are not better than whoever you cannot assist”, so we choose to assist as many special individuals as God will directs our paths.

Healthy Food

We believe that healthy food is essential for maintaining good health and improving quality of life

Respite Care

Our respite care services include a range of supportive activities designed to promote physical, mental, and emotional well-being.

Health Care

Our dedicated healthcare professionals work tirelessly to deliver compassionate care that is tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual

Education

Our education programs are designed to provide children and young adults with the skills, knowledge, and resources they need to succeed in school and beyond.

Residence Facilities

Our residence programs are designed to provide a comfortable and nurturing living space for those in need of temporary or long-term housing

Social Care

We believe that everyone deserves to feel valued and supported, and we strive to create a welcoming and inclusive community that fosters a sense of belonging and empowerment.
